Relevant bibliography

The following are a number 'good reads' on various subjects, that contain fundamental knowledge - explanations, insights and of course equations - one would need to understand how things in energy systems (and markets) work.

Power systems fundamentals

  • P. Kundur (1994). Power System Stability and Control. McGraw-Hill.
  • C.F. Price, R.R. Gibbon (1997). Statistical approach to thermal rating of overhead lines for power transmission and distribution. IEEE Proceedings C - Generation, Transmission and Distribution. 130 (5): 245-256.

Power systems standards

  • ENA ER-P27 (2020). Current rating guide for high voltage overhead lines operating in the UK distribution system.

Operations research & forecasting

  • J. Nocedal, S.J. Wright (2006). Numerical Optimisation. Springer
  • C. E. Rasmussen, C. K. I. Williams (2006). Gaussian Processes for Machine Learning. MIT Press.
  • J. Hensman, N. Fusi, N. D. Lawrence (2013). Gaussian Processes for Big Data. Conference on Uncertainty in Artificial Intelligence.
  • E. Snelson, Z. Ghahramani (2005). Sparse Gaussian Processes using Pseudo-Inputs. Neural Information Processing Systems Conference.
